# Studio32 · Digital Systems Project Brief

## What Studio32 Is

Studio32 is a digital systems studio for physical and local businesses that need to modernize how they look, capture demand and operate online.

The core promise:

> We help traditional businesses look better, capture better and operate better through premium web design, automation and practical AI applied to real business problems.

Studio32 does not sell "web pages" as a commodity. The website is the first visible layer of a broader digital system: positioning, conversion, contact flows, reservations, lead capture, intelligent assistance and simple operational infrastructure.

## What Studio32 Is Not

Studio32 is not:

- A generic marketing agency.
- A cheap landing-page factory.
- An AI hype shop.
- A SaaS product pretending every client needs a complex platform.
- A visual-only design studio with no operational thinking.

## Target Client

The primary client is a physical/local business with a strong real-world offer but weak or outdated digital infrastructure.

Typical problems:

- Old or generic website.
- Weak mobile experience.
- Poor contact or reservation flow.
- Low perceived value online compared with the real business quality.
- Manual processes that could be simplified.
- No practical use of automation or AI.
- Fragmented presence across web, Google Business Profile, WhatsApp, forms and social channels.

## Initial Verticals

- **Premium restaurants**: better presentation, reservations, menu clarity, customer trust and contact flow.
- **Hamburger/urban food brands**: strong visual identity, mobile-first conversion, menu and order/contact clarity.
- **Dental clinics**: trust, treatment clarity, first-visit capture, WhatsApp/form routing and patient confidence.
- **Architecture/interiorism studios**: editorial portfolio, authority, inquiry capture and premium service perception.

Initial commercial focus can prioritize restaurants and dental clinics, while the site should still present all four vertical demos coherently.

## Services

### Digital Presence Audit

Entry offer. Reviews the current digital presence:

- Website and mobile experience.
- Basic performance and clarity.
- Commercial structure and CTAs.
- Local SEO basics.
- Google Business Profile.
- Contact, reservation or inquiry experience.
- Visual perception.
- Automation and practical AI opportunities.

### Presence Pack

Premium web/landing for a local business:

- Clear commercial structure.
- Mobile-first build.
- WhatsApp/contact integration.
- Map/location.
- Services, products or menu.
- Gallery/portfolio.
- Form or reservation flow.
- Basic local SEO and technical setup.

### Presence + AI Pack

Premium web plus intelligent assistant:

- FAQ and lead qualification.
- Reservation/request guidance.
- Business-specific training with approved information.
- Connection to WhatsApp, email or form when useful.
- Maintenance and improvement support.

### Digital System Custom

Custom operational systems:

- Simple dashboards.
- Basic CRM.
- Internal automations.
- Inventory or reservations flows.
- Internal panels.
- Integrations with Google Sheets, Make, Zapier, n8n or similar tools.

## Business Model

Studio32 should feel premium enough to prepare the visitor for project fees plus monthly maintenance.

Internal reference ranges:

- Presence Pack: from 900 EUR.
- Presence + AI: from 1,500 EUR plus maintenance.
- Digital System Custom: from 2,500-3,000 EUR.
- Maintenance: from 99 EUR/month.
- AI/automation support: 149-399 EUR/month.

Prices do not need to be shown immediately if the visual strategy is stronger without them, but the site should never feel like a low-cost template offer.

## Brand Tone

Use language that is:

- Sober.
- Premium.
- Modern.
- Clear.
- Technical but understandable.
- Commercial but not pushy.
- Human but not casual.

Use concepts such as:

- Digital presence.
- Digital systems.
- Practical AI.
- Automation.
- Lead capture.
- Reservations.
- Customer experience.
- Operational efficiency.
- Local business modernization.
- Premium design.
- Industry-specific solutions.

Avoid:

- Empty agency phrases.
- Exaggerated growth claims.
- AI buzzword overload.
- Aggressive dominance copy.
- Juvenile tone or emojis.
- Founder-centric copy that distracts from client value.

## Strategic Priorities

1. Make the main landing clearly say **Studio32 · Digital Systems**.
2. Reframe the offering around business outcomes, not "web design".
3. Use the four demos as vertical proof of capability.
4. Make the audit the primary conversion path.
5. Keep the experience premium, fast, responsive and easy to deploy.
6. Prepare the repo for incremental improvement rather than a risky rewrite.
